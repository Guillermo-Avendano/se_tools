# Divorce Decree

**In the Matter of the Marriage of John Smith and Jane Smith**

**Decree of Divorce**

This Decree of Divorce is entered on Jan-31st, 2023 by Court Name, Location.

## Parties:

- **Petitioner:** John Smith
- **Respondent:** Jane Smith

## Financial Agreements:

1. **Spousal Support (Alimony):**
   - John Smith agrees to pay Jane Smith the amount of $1,000 per month as spousal support for a period of two years, commencing on the effective date of this divorce decree.

2. **Division of Assets and Liabilities:**
   - The residential property located at 123 Main Street, Cityville, is awarded to John Smith. He assumes responsibility for the outstanding mortgage balance.

## Other Financial Obligations:

- **Bankruptcy Disclosure:**
   - John Smith discloses that he has not filed for bankruptcy within the past 10 years.

## Finalization:

This divorce decree is final and binding upon the parties involved. Both parties acknowledge that they have received independent legal advice and fully understand the implications of the financial agreements outlined herein.
