**Loan Agreement**

This Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on February 7, 2024, by and between:

**Lender:** Capital Bank, a financial institution organized and existing under the laws of the State of New York, with its principal place of business located at 123 Main Street, New York, NY 10001,

and

**Borrower:** John Smith, an individual residing at 456 Oak Street, Cityville, State, ZIP Code.

**Loan Details:**

1. **Loan Amount:** The Lender agrees to lend to the Borrower the principal sum of Fifty Thousand Dollars ($50,000.00) ("Loan Amount"). This amount will be disbursed to the Borrower upon signing of this Agreement and will be used for home improvement purposes.

2. **Interest Rate:** The Loan shall accrue interest at an annual rate of 6%, calculated on the outstanding principal balance and compounded monthly. This interest rate is fixed for the duration of the loan term.

3. **Purpose of Loan:** The Borrower intends to use the Loan Amount for home improvement purposes, as outlined in the Home Improvement Estimates document attached hereto as Exhibit A. These improvements include roof repairs, kitchen renovation, bathroom remodeling, and exterior painting, with estimated costs detailed in the document.

4. **Repayment Terms:**
   - The Borrower shall repay the Loan Amount to the Lender in monthly installments commencing on March 1, 2024, and continuing on the 1st day of each month thereafter. The term of the loan shall be 5 years, with 60 monthly installments.
   - Each installment payment shall be applied first to any accrued and unpaid interest, then to the outstanding principal balance of the Loan. The Borrower shall make payments to the Lender via automatic bank transfer from the Borrower's designated account.

5. **Prepayment:** The Borrower shall have the option to prepay the Loan, in whole or in part, at any time without penalty. Any prepayments made shall be applied first to any accrued and unpaid interest, then to the outstanding principal balance of the Loan.

6. **Late Payments:** In the event that any installment payment is not received by the Lender within [number of days] days of its due date, the Borrower shall be considered in default under this Agreement, and a late fee of [Late Fee Amount] shall be assessed for each late payment.

7. **Collateral:** The Borrower offers the residential property located at 789 Elm Avenue, Cityville, State, ZIP Code, as collateral security for the Loan. The estimated value of the property is Two Hundred Thousand Dollars ($200,000.00), with an outstanding mortgage balance of One Hundred Twenty Thousand Dollars ($120,000.00). This property will serve as security for the repayment of the Loan in the event of default by the Borrower.

8. **Default and Remedies:** In the event of default by the Borrower under this Agreement, the Lender shall have the right to exercise any remedies available under applicable law, including but not limited to acceleration of the Loan, foreclosure on the collateral, and recovery of costs and expenses incurred in enforcing its rights hereunder. The Borrower shall be provided with notice of default and an opportunity to cure the default before any remedies are exercised by the Lender.

9. **Governing Law and Jurisdiction:** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York. Any disputes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association. The parties agree to submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of the State of New York for any legal proceedings arising from or related to this Agreement.

10. **Confidentiality:** The parties agree to keep the terms and conditions of this Agreement confidential and not to disclose them to any third party without the prior written consent of the other party, except as required by law or regulation.

11. **Assignment:** Neither party may assign its rights or obligations under this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other party, except that the Lender may assign its rights under this Agreement to any affiliate or successor in interest without the Borrower's consent.

12. **Miscellaneous:** This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether written or oral, relating thereto. This Agreement may not be modified or amended except in writing signed by both parties. The Borrower acknowledges that he has read and understands the terms and conditions of this Agreement and agrees to be bound by them.
